Our Story

Rooted in craftsmanship. Driven by care.

EverGreen Landscapes began as a weekend passion project and grew through word-of-mouth into a trusted team of designers, horticulturists, and builders. Since 2012, we’ve transformed hundreds of outdoor spaces—front yards, backyards, rooftops, and everything in between.

We believe a great landscape should be beautiful, functional, and sustainable. That’s why we design with native plants where possible, integrate smart irrigation, and build with durable materials that stand the test of time.
